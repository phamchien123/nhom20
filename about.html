ArB Tree Care

ArB Tree Care Ltd is a Staffordshire based Tree Surgery and Forestry Company founded by Anthony Bethell in 2002. Anthony, a forest graduate, full member of the Institute of Chartered Foresters and a registered Chartered Arboriculturist, is currently the Managing Director of the company, although he can often be found out on site with the rest of the team.We undertake all aspects of tree work, from dangerous tree removals and felling, tree pruning and crown reductions to tree planting, guarding and fencing. In addition to this, we provide tree and woodland management services and a consultancy services.

 

Some of our existing clients include members of the public, landowners and country estates owners, farmers, councils, corporate businesses, golf clubs, educational establishments, wildlife trusts and utility companies.Regardless of how big or small a contract is, we ensure that it is treated with the upmost respect and complete the job to the best of our ability, in line with BS3998 industry standards.

Safety At ArB

ArB has a full Health & Safety Policy which can be viewed upon client’s request. Risk assessments and method statements are undertaken prior to commencing any work to ensure the safety of our employees and members of the public. Copies of these can be provided to the client upon their request. The Company is a member of Construction Line and conforms to all LOLER and PUWER regulations and maintains stringent records of all equipment and machinery inspections. ArB Tree Care carries an Employees Liability Insurance and a Public and Product Liability Insurance Policy of £10,000,000 which covers all the services we offer.

British Armed Forces
ArB is a proud supporter of the British Armed Forces, and actively sponsors the Armed Forces Bikers (RCN: 1147967). The AFB is a UK-based charity, which aims to support former serving members of the armed forces in charitable need as a result of injury or other harmful experience suffered during their service. In addition, we are registered with the Armed Forces Covenant signatory and are rated silver by the Defence Employer Recognition Scheme (ERS).
